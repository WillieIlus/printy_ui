## Website Descriptions

### Short (160 characters)
Get instant printing quotes in Kenya. Find nearby print shops, compare prices for business cards, flyers, and posters, and request quotes in one place.

### Medium (300–500 characters)
Printy connects buyers with print shops across Kenya. Browse by location or product, compare prices for business cards, flyers, posters, and more. Get instant quotes, customize paper and finishing options, and request formal quotes—all in one place. Whether you need 100 business cards or 10,000 flyers, Printy helps you find the right shop and the right price.

### Long Company Description
Printy is a marketplace that helps people find nearby print shops and compare printing services in Kenya. We make it easy to discover local printers, compare prices for business cards, flyers, posters, brochures, and more, and request quotes without the back-and-forth.

Unlike generic directories, Printy focuses on print. Shops list their products and pricing so you can see estimates before you reach out. You can filter by location, browse by product type, and add items to a quote draft to compare options across multiple shops.

Printy is built for Kenyan businesses, freelancers, and anyone who needs professional printing. We help shops reach more customers and help buyers find the right printer at the right price.
